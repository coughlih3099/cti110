Magic: The Gathering

Magic: the Gathering is the best game ever created

It was created by Richard Garfield and launched in 1993.

Magic: The Gathering is a card game. Two players assume the role of a "Planeswalker" and battle each other.

There are a multitude of different ways to play Magic, called formats, but the most popular way to play is a format called Commander.

Commander, unlike other official formats, is four player; each player creates a 100-card, singleton (that means you can only have one copy of each non-basic land), and battles in a free-for-all match.

Unfortunately, Wizards of the Coast (the company that runs MTG), hates it's playerbase and doesn't get what it takes to run a digital client; so the premier way to play Magic, MTG Arena, doesn't have access to commander

The Arena analog of Commander is called Brawl which, coincidentally, is the way I play Magic the most
